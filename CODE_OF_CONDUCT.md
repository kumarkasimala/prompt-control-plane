# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: keep discussion technical, respectful, and focused on improving the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Respect different levels of experience.
- Keep feedback about ideas, designs, and code.
- Avoid sharing private data, customer data, secrets, or confidential implementation details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private or confidential information.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to keep the project useful and professional.
